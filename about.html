Company Overview

The Innovative energy conservation solutions

IECS are a leading independent full-service commercial energy consultancy based in Mohali Punjab/Chandigarh.

We work alongside businesses of all sizes and take the time to help organisations evaluate and understand their energy usage and expenditure. The IECS then create bespoke energy savings proposals that are specifically suited to your organisational requirements.

IECS offer all our clients a fully managed end to end bespoke energy consultancy service that includes advice and assistance to help your organisation monitor, manage & control your energy more efficiently via our specialist sustainability team and providing your organisation with the best value for money.

IECS recognises that its operations have a direct and indirect impact on the natural and socio-economic environments, and believes in operating in an ethical manner for the benefit of all.

IECS are committed to conducting business in a responsible manner in accordance with its legal and regulatory obligations, and uphold all applicable industry codes of conduct.

IECS We are working to make a greater contribution to society through corporate social responsibility by:

  • Playing an active role in supporting local communities and organisations in addressing social and environmental issue
  • Offering services that have a positive environmental and socio-economic impact

IECS are experts in assisting organisations comply with any legislative requirements that may be imposed upon them such as ISO 50001-2018, ESOS (Energy Savings Opportunity Scheme), CCA’s (Climate Change Agreements) & CRC’s (Carbon Reduction Commitments).

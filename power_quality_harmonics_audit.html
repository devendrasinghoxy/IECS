Power Quality & Harmonics Audit

Power Quality Audits Identifies Power Quality Issues. This will further help you to address reliability and stability concerns before they actually impact on your facilities performance and more important on bottom line of organization. All electrical devices work properly without any failure, if pure sinusoidal power with minimum harmonics in voltage & current is feed. Power Quality Study determines the quality of electrical power i.e. harmonics, transient and distorted electrical parameters from source to load end or vice-versa. With Distorted values in Voltage and Current in electrical power, an electrical device or load may be malfunction, fail prematurely or may not operate. There are many ways in which electric power can be of poor quality and many more causes of such poor quality power. The quality of electrical power may be described as a set of values of parameters, such as:

  • Continuity of Service
  • Variation in voltage magnitude
  • Inrushin currents
  • Harmonics content in the weave forms for AC power

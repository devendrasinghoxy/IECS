Pumping System Audit

Pumping system audits is a diagnostic tool to identify excessive energy consumption in any kind of pumping system. Audit in pumps can often lead to a 20-25% reduction in pumping power, which translates into significant potential savings for your installation, and a little extra relief for the environment.An Energy Audit is performed by IECS specialists based on measurements. It focuses on the amount of material the pump is handling and flow variations during a relatively short and well-defined period. IECS specialist sets up various measuring equipment to measure and record the following:

  • Flow, pressure, and power, for pump efficiency study
  • Fine tuning of pumps to match them with system resistance
  • Assessment of existingcapacity control method on pump
  • Suggestions for installation of higher efficiency pumps
  • Installation of VFD wherever economically viable
  • Present system analysis and compatibility with present requirement
